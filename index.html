Mapping the 2020 presidential election results

By Kunle Falayi

This map shows the state and county-level results of the 2020 Presidential Election. This map is modelled after the choropleth style used in this New York Times piece. Both maps were created using mapbox.

Click on any state to see result. You may also zoom in to see county-level results

This graduated point map is another version of the same election result inspired by a similar map created by the New York Times after the 2012 Presidential Election.
